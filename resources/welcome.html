Welcome to vise!

vise is largely keyboard driven. There are two "modes", the normal mode for general browsing and the insert mode for editing text in form fields. Below is a cheatsheat for the important keyboard shortcuts in both modes. For the complete list of keyboard shortcuts as well as other customization possibilities, see config.yaml.
